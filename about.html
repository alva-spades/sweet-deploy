About Us

With precision to detail and bespoke services, our work and how we make your experience intimate and unforgettable for your loved ones, is leveraged by our passion, expertise and professionalism. You can rely on us to create euphoric experiences on occasions that matter to you most and those who matter to you. Established in 2021, Sweet Bunny was designed to offer our sensitive clients personalized service through creativity and uniqueness based on their preference, taste and intimacy of the occasion. At Sweet Bunny, we understand words at times can never describe sentiments attached to occasions and people, but with our unique way of curating our events coupled with tailored treats from our offering in the form of baked goods, sweet treats. We make every experience unique, personal and memorable.
